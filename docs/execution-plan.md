## Risk Mitigation

### Technical Risks

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-------------|--------|------------|
| API downtime | Medium | High | Implement retry logic, have fallback providers |
| Poor voice quality | Low | High | Extensive testing before launch, quick iteration |
| Webhook failures | Medium | Medium | Log all webhooks, manual recovery process |
| Rate limiting | Low | Medium | Batch processing, respect rate limits |

### Business Risks

| Risk | Probability | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|-------------|--------|------------|
| Low connection rate | Medium | High | Test different call times, improve number recognition |
| High DNC requests | Low | Medium | Train AI to handle gracefully, honor immediately |
| Poor conversion | Medium | High | Continuous A/B testing, human follow-up for HOT leads |
| Ad account ban | Low | High | Maintain compliance, have backup ad accounts |
